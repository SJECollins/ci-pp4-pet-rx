Learn More About PetRx

PetRx is an easy to use platform that provides an extra layer of safety and consistency in your practice. Easily tailor PetRx to your own clinic's drug protocols so that you can avoid human error and be sure of reliability when it comes to prescribing medications for your patients.

Benefits:

  • Ease of use
  • Consistency
  • Safety

Ease of Use

PetRx takes away the tedious job of calculating dosages. Once your practice's preferred dosage for a medication is set and animal's weight is registered, all you need to do is go to a patient's file, select "Add Prescription" and select the drug you wish to prescribe. PetRx handles the calculation for you.

PetRx saves all prescriptions to a patient's file for easy review. And every prescription you create will be saved to your own profile for you to review whenever you need.

Consistency

As all the calculations are done by PetRx, you can be sure that all your staff are following your practice's drug dosing protocols.

With all prescriptions saved to an animal's profile, you can quickly review their medication history for any irregularities.

Safety

Prevent human error in medication dosages by setting your practice's dosing protocols as you add a drug to the database.

Record every prescription on an animal's record as it is calculated so that reviewing their prescription history is quick and easy, and you can spot any irregularities.

Include relevant warnings specific to your practice for each drug, which your staff can review in the detailed drug view.
